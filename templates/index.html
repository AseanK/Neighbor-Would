Connect with Your Neighbors and Stay Updated

Welcome to NeighborWould, the community platform where you can easily connect with your neighbors and stay informed about local events. Whether you're organizing a garage sale, throwing a party, or hosting a community gathering, NeighborWould provides a convenient way to share and discover events in your neighborhood.

With NeighborWould, you can explore a wide range of events happening in your area. Stay informed about upcoming events and plan your schedule accordingly.

Never miss out on the exciting happenings in your community again!

How does it work?

  • Browse local events effortlessly
  • Supports both Month calendar view and List calendar view
    • Month view: Displays a traditional calendar view
    • List view: Presents events as a well-organized list
    • Both views provides starting time and event titles
  • Click on an event to view detailed information
    • Shows starting and ending date and time
    • Includes a description with event location
  • Create local events effortlessly
    • Select the date to start the event
    • Supports multi-day events
    • Customize the event display color
